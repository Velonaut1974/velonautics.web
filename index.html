The Regulatory Collision

EU-ETS (Mass-Based)

Metric: Absolute CO2 Emissions

The EU Emissions Trading System operates on a 'Cap & Trade' principle. Ship operators must surrender one EUA for every ton of verified CO2. The goal is volume reduction through financial incentives.

FuelEU Maritime (Intensity)

Metric: GHG Intensity (gCO2e/MJ)

FuelEU focuses on energy quality. It sets limits on the greenhouse gas intensity of fuels used, rewarding renewables through pooling. Strategic fuel mix orchestration is essential to avoid heavy penalties.
